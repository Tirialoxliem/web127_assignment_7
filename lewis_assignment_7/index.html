Welcome to The Lancaster Zoo

Welcome everybody to the Lancaster Zoo. With over 100 enclosures, on 200 acres of land, you will have fun all day for the whole family. Whether you like to hear the roar of a lion, growl of a bear, or howl of a wolf, you will certainly have some special moments. We are open 365 days a year. We cater to individuals, and up to very large groups. We have on site free parking. We have indoor celebration facilities to rent for birthdays, and other special occasions. We were voted as the top attraction in Pennsylvania, and a top 5 zoo in the United States.
