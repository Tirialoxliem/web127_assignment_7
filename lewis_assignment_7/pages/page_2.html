Meet Our Zoo Animals

We have over one thousand animals in our facilities. Below you will see a number of our animal guests that live on our premises. Our animals are fed on strict diets that are for their special needs, and they are treated by the top animal doctors in the country. Our zookeepers are highly trained, and go through yearly intensive training and learning, to get recertified yearly to care for our animals. You will be wowed by our many animals, such as the lions, tigers, elephants, bears, hippos, rhinos, wolves, giraffes, gorillas, monkeys, and many more. Do not forget to stop in our petting area for some of our animals.
