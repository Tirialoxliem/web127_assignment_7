Don't Forget About Our Aquarium

While you are here, remember to check out our aquarium which is included with your admission. Catch one of our dolphin shows for a big splash, and an opportunity to feed them. For you shark lovers, check out our shark feeding shows, which occur many times a day, times are written on the brochures. You will have the opportunity to be up close to see the hammerheads, tiger, mako, and the most feared of all, the great whites. Our state-of-the-art facilities are four stories and open all day. Our aquarium is ranked in the top three in our country, so enjoy the facilities.
